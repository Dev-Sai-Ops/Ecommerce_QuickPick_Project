Refund Policy

At QuickPick, we strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. We understand that sometimes things don't work out — and that’s okay. Please review our refund and return policy below.

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date.

To qualify for a return:

  • Item must be in original condition and packaging
  • Clothing must be unworn, unwashed, with all original tags
  • Electronics must be unused or in like-new condition with all original accessories
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required

Non-returnable items:

  • Opened or used earbuds/headphones or personal care electronics
  • Final sale or clearance items

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, you will be notified by email. If your refund is approved, it will be processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Late or missing refunds?

  • Check your bank or credit card account again
  • Contact your credit card company (processing times vary)
  • Still need help? Email us at support@quickpick.com

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged upon delivery. If you need an exchange, email support@quickpick.com with your order number.

Electronics Warranty

Most electronics come with a manufacturer’s warranty (usually 6–12 months). If a device malfunctions during this time:

  • Contact us for warranty support
  • You may also reach out directly to the manufacturer
  • Warranty claims usually require proof of purchase

QuickPick
Mumbai
