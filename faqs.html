Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about orders, payments, delivery, and more.

Q: When will I receive my order?
A: Shipped within 24 hours, delivered in 3–7 days.

Q: Do you offer cash on delivery (COD)?
A: Yes, COD is available for select locations and products during checkout.

Q: Can I change my shipping address?
A: Yes, before dispatch. Contact support.

Q: Payment methods?
A: UPI, cards, and popular wallets.

Q: Requesting a refund?
A: Email support with your order ID.

Q: Charged but no confirmation?
A: Send payment reference via email for help.

Q: Return policy?
A: 7-day return for unused items.

Q: How to cancel an order?
A: Contact us before dispatch.

Q: Cancellation fees?
A: None, if canceled early.

Q: Contact support?
A: Email support@quickpick.com or call +91 9876543210

Q: Delivery to my area?
A: Enter your pincode at checkout to confirm.

Q: Minimum order value?
A: No, order any amount you like!
